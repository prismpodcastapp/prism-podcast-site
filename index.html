Audio,
Uninterrupted.

Prism is the podcast player that gets out of your way. No social feeds, no algorithm, no ads. Just a beautifully crafted offline-first experience for Android.

Get on Google Play

Find Anything, Instantly.

Powered by the iTunes API, Prism gives you access to virtually every podcast in existence. Search by show name, author, or keywords. Browse curated "Top 25" charts for any country to discover your next obsession.

Built for the Commute.

Prism is Offline-First by design. We manage your storage intelligently so your library is always ready when you step out the door. No buffering, no data usage on the go. Just hit play.

Modern Audio Engine.

Built on top of Google's Media3 & ExoPlayer libraries. Enjoy variable speed playback (0.5x to 3x), silence skipping, sleep timers, and perfect system volume integration.

Zero Monetization.

Prism is a passion project, not a startup. We have no investors to please and no ads to serve. Your listening habits are your business, not ours.
